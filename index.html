How to Play & About

Dots and Boxes was invented by French mathematician Édouard Lucas in 1889, who called it La Pipopipette.

Rules

  1. Players take turns drawing one line between two adjacent dots.
  2. Complete the 4th side of a box to claim it and take another turn.
  3. The player with the most boxes when the grid is full wins.

💡 Tip: Avoid adding the 3rd side to any box — it gives your opponent a free point!
